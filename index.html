How to play

Use the boxing glove in your box to destroy the box-producing factory.

Click a conveyor belt to attack in that lane. You can hold for continuous attacking.

You will encounter various kinds of boxes, each with a special effect when destroyed.

Letting a box through the conveyor will take 1 HP from you, except for explosive and deadly boxes, they can pass safely.
